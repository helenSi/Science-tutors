Teaching Experience

 I have ten years experience in teaching Biology in Secondary School and Further Education College. Mainly GCSE and A level standard on an Access to Higher Education Health & Health Science and BTEC Forensic Science course.
 I have taught all aspects of Biology from Genetics, Health & Disease to Anatomy.  

I also teach Basic and Intermediate Computing including: Microsoft Office Products Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and File Management.

I completed my PGCE in 2006. I have a BSc and PhD in Microbiology from the University of Surrey. I have also 7 years experience as a Scientific Advisor at a US law Firm in Texas and a Postdoctoral Researcher in Molecular Biology at the University of Leicester. 

Teaching Style

I have a very friendly approach. Biology is a fascinating subject and so it is a joy to teach - the only problem is that there is so much to learn!
I like to use activities and visual aids to help teach the more complex concepts with questions and discussions to reinforce understanding.  I believe learning is a social activity and I try to make both the learning and teaching process as enjoyable as possible.
